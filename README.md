# 📖 Executive Summary

ClariPulse™ is an enterprise-grade Clinical AI product that combines predictive analytics, Explainable AI (XAI), executive intelligence, and Responsible AI governance into a unified clinical decision support ecosystem.

The product demonstrates how modern healthcare organizations can operationalize trustworthy AI through transparent prediction pipelines, governance controls, benchmarked machine learning models, and executive-ready reporting.

---

# 🎯 Product Objectives

* Deliver AI-powered clinical risk prediction
* Improve transparency through SHAP explainability
* Enable Responsible AI governance
* Support executive decision-making
* Benchmark competing machine learning models
* Generate enterprise-ready reports
* Provide clinician-friendly patient-level predictions
